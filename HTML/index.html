Building a powerful web around you

Speed-of-light communication and faster-than-a-horse transportation are fairly recent capabilities that have allowed us to overcome many geographic barriers. But digital technologies that help us think about an expanded spatial world, such as GIS, remote sensing, GPS and location-based services, are much more recent than those technologies which expanded our ability to act in the world, and they are used little. On the whole, our accommodation to our modern, expanded realm of action is limited by a spatial awareness that hasn’t advanced much beyond the range of our seeing and hearing and our memories of places we have been.


The Spatial Web Project has created a revolutionary new technology that will enable us to run or execute massive chunks of code hyper-locally. The users in the proximity can take advantage of these codes that will be specific to the place that they run in. The locally executed codes and data by architectural nature will run significantly faster than a typical server hosted website or Data Base; this allows an opportunity for more computationally enhanced functions, local to the user.








TARGETED IMMEDIATE APPLICATIONS







Powerful local applications

As the code executions is carried at a much closer range in terms of bits and bytes, this enables faster processing. Bridging the gap between comparably slow web apps to faster native apps, spacial apps can solve the problem of explosive number of apps in apps stores. By providing application at the places they are needed, giving them a contextual value.

ONE VARIABLE CHIP

These dynamic cubes represent the presence of any device in the system which opens up possibilities for IOT (internet of things), within the specially closed environments and systems. Our team is attempting to build what we call the one variable chip. A sticker with enabled GPS brodcasting, which can be assigned to varaible in the platform, can be used to code dynamic spatial codes arround it.

POWEFUL LOCAL SEARCH

Due to the architecture of the system local discovery and search within certain radius becomes incredibly fast and easy. With search results local to you, you could search for a particular event, medicine, food or any commodity in your locality to find location specific results. Also the spacial app discovery becomes easy.



Direct communication and application acces between devices

As Explained above the Dynamic cubes can also communicate with each other. With the presence of cashes, cookies and cloud storage, you can run a local code within your dynamic code to communicate directly between the local devices. Usually, though there are presence of devices all around us, it is not possible to directly communicate with them unless you know the specifics of the device such as the emails, cell phone numbers etc. This direct communication can allow devices to communicate with each other locally or preform certain local functions.
